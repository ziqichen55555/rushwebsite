Refund Policy

Booking Cancellations

Standard Bookings

Our refund policy for standard bookings is as follows:

  • More than 48 hours before pickup: Full refund
  • 24-48 hours before pickup: 75% refund
  • 12-24 hours before pickup: 50% refund
  • Less than 12 hours before pickup: No refund
Premium and Luxury Vehicles

Due to high demand for our premium and luxury vehicles, a different cancellation policy applies:

  • More than 7 days before pickup: Full refund
  • 3-7 days before pickup: 75% refund
  • 1-3 days before pickup: 50% refund
  • Less than 24 hours before pickup: No refund

Booking Modifications

Change of Dates

Modifications to booking dates are subject to the following conditions:

  • Modifications made more than 48 hours before pickup: No fee
  • Modifications made less than 48 hours before pickup: $30 AUD change fee
  • All modifications are subject to vehicle availability
  • If the new rental period is shorter than the original booking, the above cancellation policy applies to the difference
Change of Vehicle

Changes to the booked vehicle type are subject to the following:

  • Upgrade to a more expensive vehicle category: Pay only the difference in rate
  • Downgrade to a less expensive vehicle category: Refund based on cancellation policy for the difference
  • All vehicle changes are subject to availability

Early Returns

If you return the vehicle earlier than the scheduled return date:

  • No refund will be provided for unused rental days if the return is within 24 hours of the scheduled return
  • Early returns with more than 24 hours remaining may be eligible for a partial refund (50% of the remaining rental value)
  • All early returns must be communicated to us at least 24 hours in advance to be eligible for any refund

No-Shows

If you fail to pick up the vehicle at the scheduled time without prior notification:

  • The reservation will be held for a maximum of 2 hours after the scheduled pickup time
  • After this period, the booking will be considered a "no-show" and no refund will be provided
  • The full rental amount will be charged to the credit card used for booking

Refund Process

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund for a cancelled booking, please:

  1. Log in to your Rush Car Rental account
  2. Navigate to "My Bookings"
  3. Select the booking you wish to cancel
  4. Click on "Cancel Booking" and follow the prompts

Alternatively, you can contact our customer service team at:

  • Email: refunds@rushcarrental.com
  • Phone: 1800 RUSH CAR (1800 7874 227)
Refund Timeframe

Once a refund is approved:

  • Credit card refunds will be processed within 3-5 business days
  • The funds may take an additional 5-10 business days to appear in your account, depending on your financial institution
