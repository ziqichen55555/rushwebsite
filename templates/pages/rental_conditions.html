Rental Conditions

Driver Requirements

Age Requirements
  • Minimum age: 21 years old
  • Drivers under 25 may be subject to a Young Driver Surcharge
  • Maximum age: 75 years old (may vary by vehicle type)
  • Seniors aged 70-75 must provide a valid medical certificate confirming fitness to drive
License Requirements
  • All drivers must hold a valid driver's license for at least 12 months
  • International visitors must have an International Driving Permit along with their original license
  • License must be presented at the time of vehicle pickup

Reservation and Payment

Booking and Payment Policy
  • Credit card used for booking must be presented at pickup
  • Valid credit card required for security deposit
  • Full payment is required at the time of vehicle pickup
  • Accepted payment methods: Visa, MasterCard, American Express
Security Deposit
  • A security deposit (bond) will be held on your credit card during the rental period
  • Standard vehicles: $500 AUD
  • Premium vehicles: $1,000 AUD
  • Luxury vehicles: $2,000 AUD
  • The deposit will be refunded upon vehicle return, subject to vehicle condition

Vehicle Usage

Prohibited Usage
  • Off-road driving is strictly prohibited
  • Vehicles must not be used for towing without prior authorization
  • No smoking in any of our vehicles
  • Pets are not allowed in vehicles unless a pet-friendly vehicle is specifically booked
  • Vehicles must not be used for commercial purposes
Geographical Restrictions
  • Vehicles must not be driven outside of Australia
  • Some vehicle categories have restrictions on unsealed roads
  • Traveling to certain remote areas may require prior authorization

Additional Fees and Charges

Additional Driver Fee

Additional drivers must be registered at the time of pickup. A fee of $5 AUD per day applies per additional driver.

Late Return Fee

A late return fee of $50 AUD plus the daily rate will apply for vehicles returned after the agreed return time without prior notification.

Fuel Policy

Vehicles are provided with a full tank of fuel and must be returned with a full tank. A refueling fee will apply if the vehicle is not returned with a full tank.

Cleaning Fee

A cleaning fee of $100-$300 AUD may apply if the vehicle is returned in an excessively dirty condition.

Insurance and Liability

Standard Coverage

All rentals include standard insurance with an excess/deductible amount that varies by vehicle category.

Excess Reduction Options

We offer several excess reduction options to reduce your liability in case of damage or accident:

  • Standard Protection: Included in rental rate (excess applies)
  • Premium Protection: Additional daily fee, reduces excess by 50%
  • Full Protection: Additional daily fee, eliminates excess completely
What's Not Covered

Insurance does not cover:

  • Damage to the undercarriage, roof, or windscreen
  • Water damage
  • Damage resulting from negligence or violation of rental terms
  • Interior damage
  • Loss or damage to personal belongings
